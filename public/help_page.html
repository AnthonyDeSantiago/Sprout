Getting Started
Register
  • Access the registration page on our website.
  • Enter personal details including your first and last name, email, and date of birth.
  • Provide your physical address information.
  • Create and confirm a secure password for your account.
  • Select a default profile picture or upload a custom image.
  • Choose and answer two security questions to enhance account security.
  • Finalize your account setup by clicking the 'Create Account' button.
  • After registration, a link to the login page is provided for easy navigation.
Forgot Password
  • Use the 'Forgot Password' feature found on the login page when necessary.
  • Enter your registered email and username to verify your identity.
  • Answer your security questions as an additional security check.
  • Create and confirm a new password for your account.
  • Look for error messages, if any, to correct your inputs.
  • Submit the form to reset your password successfully.
  • Receive a confirmation message and then return to the login page to access your account with the new password.

Transactions

Manage financial transactions effectively within Sprout Financials. Users can access and edit the Chart of Accounts, review and process User Journals, and create new Journal Entries with ease:

  • Chart of Accounts: View active and deactivated accounts, add new ones, or edit existing details with options to save or deactivate. Administrative users can manage account status and order.
  • User Journals: Oversee journal entries through stages of pending, rejected, and approved, with options to review, approve, or reject. Each entry contains comprehensive financial data for accurate record-keeping.
  • New Journal Entries: Navigate to the journal entry section to select accounts, input debit and credit amounts, and add descriptions. Attach documents, provide a journal description, and submit your entry or use the reset button to clear all fields.

With tools for detailed oversight and management, Sprout Financials simplifies transaction handling, from initial entry to final approval.

Reports

Generate and analyze a range of financial reports in Sprout Financials by following these steps:

  • Select Date Range: Choose the start and end date to define the period for your report.
  • Load Reports: Click the 'Load Reports' button to display reports for the selected dates.
  • Report Type Selection: Use the dropdown menu to select the type of report you need, such as Trial Balance, Income Statement, Balance Sheet, or Retained Earnings Statement. You can also choose to generate all reports.
  • Download, Print, Email: Once the report is generated, you have the option to download, print, or email it. For emailing, simply enter the email address and click 'Email'.
  • Report Tables: View your reports organized in tables with clear headings, allowing for easy analysis of Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenues, and Expenses.
  • Time-Stamped: Each report is time-stamped, capturing the exact moment of generation for your records.

With these tools, Sprout Financials provides a comprehensive view of your financial standings through detailed reports.
